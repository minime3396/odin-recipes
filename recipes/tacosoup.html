Taco Soup

Description

This meal is my version of taco soup. It is a bit more picky child friendly than some of the other versions. I eliminated most veggies.

You can play around with this recipe and add more or less of anything to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 4LBS hamburger
  • 3 cans of dark red kidney beans
  • 2 cans of rotel tomatoes(and juice)
  • 3-4 packets of taco seasoning
  • 2T beef base
  • 1-2 ranch seasoning packets

Instructions

  • Brown meat in a pan
  • Dump the rest of the ingredients into the crock pot and stir
  • Strain meat and add to crock pot
  • Turn on low for 6 hours or high for 4
  • Serve with any garnishes and/or tortilla chips
