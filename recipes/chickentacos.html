Chicken Tacos

Description

This is my favorite meal. I love how easy and fast it is to make. My sister actually taught me this recipe.

This meal is low on prep work. Easy to dump and go. Makes excellent left overs.

Ingredients

  • 3Lbs chicken
  • 2 Taco seasoning packets
  • 1 16 OZ jar of salsa
  • Taco shells
    •

    Steps

    1. Place the 3LBS of chicken in the bottom of the crockpot
    2. Dump the taco seasoning evenly on the chicken
    3. Pour the jar of salsa over the chicken breast
    4. Place the lid on the crock pot and set to high for 3-4 hours
    5. Shred the chicken and set crockpot to warm for 30 minutes
    6. Serve on shells
