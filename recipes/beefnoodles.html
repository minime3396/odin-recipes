Beef and Noodles

Description

My favorite thing about cooking is finding easy dump in recipes. Take away all of my brain effort please! I especially enjoy this meal in the colder months. Something about it just makes sure you are heated up from the inside.

This meal goes great with mashed potatoes and corn. You can easily increase the quantities of this meal to better meet your serving needs.

Ingredients

  • Beef broth(or base)
  • Egg noodles
  • Au jus gravy packet
  • Ranch seasoning packet
  • Stew meat
  • Various seasonings, salt, pepper, garlic, onion powder for example

Instructions

  1. Combine seasonings and beef broth into a pot
  2. Bring the beef broth to a boil, if using base bring water to a boil and add in base
  3. Add in stew meat and let boil until tender
  4. Add noodles and let boil until cooked thoroughly
  5. serve
